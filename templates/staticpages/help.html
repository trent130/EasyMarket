Help Center

1. Account Creation

Follow these steps to create your account on the student marketplace:

  1. Visit the homepage at Easymarket.com.
  2. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner.
  3. Fill in the required information, including:
    • Username (alphanumeric, 6-20 characters)
    • Email address (valid email format)
    • Password (at least 8 characters, including a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols)

2. Security Tips

Protect yourself from scams and keep your information safe by:

  • Never share your password with anyone.
  • Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true.
  • Use strong and unique passwords for your account.
  • Only communicate with other users through the platform's messaging system.
  • Report any suspicious activity to the support team.

3. Troubleshooting

Forgot password

If you forgot your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. We will send you a link to reset your password.

Payment issues

If you encounter problems with payment, please ensure you have entered the correct information and sufficient funds are available. If the issue persists, contact our support team.

4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the difference between a buyer and a seller?
Buyers purchase items or services listed on the marketplace. Sellers create listings and sell items or services.
How do I leave feedback for a seller?
Once you complete a transaction, you can leave feedback for the seller on their profile page.
What if I have a dispute with another user?
Please contact our support team who will help you resolve the dispute.

5. Advanced Features

Reviews and ratings

Read reviews and ratings from other users to make informed decisions before buying.

Dispute resolution

We offer a secure dispute resolution process to ensure fair transactions for everyone.

Secure payments

Use our secure payment methods to protect your financial information.
